About School

  • To universalize secondary education with quality teaching Swami Vivekanand Govt. Model School, Arain is one from the 186 educationally backward blocks identified by the central government of India in Rajasthan. SVGMS, Arain is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E).
  • Swami Vivekananda Government Model School (SVGMS) concentrates on staying at par with the infrastructure and facilities of the same and higher standard as that of a K.V. school with stipulations on pupil-teacher ratio, holistic educational environment, appropriate curriculum, integrated education for the disabled, girl child education, use of ICT (Information and Communication Technology) in schools.
  • SVGMS to ensure holistic development, our schools will aim at imparting students with knowledge, discipline, a sense of responsibility and confidence. Model school has a mission to continue to do what leading institutes have always done: develop this school and the students with integrity and values; to give our students the best opportunities and the best all-round education. The purpose of education is character building. Children have wonderful learning capabilities and can be shaped into persons of excellence by their teachers.
