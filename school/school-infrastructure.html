School Infrastructure

Infrastructure: That Makes A Difference

“Here….the souls meet solace…”

Infrastructure of any institute is planned and constructed to cater for different needs of the human resources there. As an educational institute is an R&D center of a society to nurture the skills and values in the students there so its infrastructure matters a lot.

SVGMS is a perfect blend of open to air and covered amenities.

The school premises consist of nearly 40000 square meter of land with convenient approach from road and railways. Its 75% area is dedicated to open to air infra like lawns, gardens, outdoor games’ courts and playgrounds. The lush green lawn welcomes at the very entrance of the premises. Multi colored buntings make this welcome more ethnic and warmer. The shady trees and evergreen flower beds catch the eyes of the beholders and sooth their sights. The work for different courts and playgrounds is in full swing to provide the novice players such an opportunity to be champions.

Each classroom is spacious enough to have comfortable seating conditions for the students. The seating furniture is for maximum 40 students in every classroom. The rooms are fitted with appropriate number of electric equipments like fans and tube-lights. Proper ventilation with natural day light keeps the rooms more hygienic and cozy. Thanks to the sweepers and peons, as they are dutifully engaged in cleaning them on daily basis.

Our institute has state-of-the art labs for different subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. Trained and dedicated lab assistants are always there to keep them functional for the maximum participation of the students and get the learning more practical and functional rather than theoretical.

With the aim to get the students computer savvy at the earliest, our school has set up nearly 25 computers with two instructors in school computer labs. The RKCL initiative of CLICK program is running successfully here so that the students can qualify RSCIT exam at very early age. They are confident when it comes to be something technically challenging because of their rich experience in computer practical.

Getting the little brains interested and engaged for future smart machines, ATAL TINKERING LABS are set up in certain number of schools in India by Central Government and NITI AYOG. Fortunately, we are one of those schools to have such a modern and techno-friendly set up here. The machine learning and artificial intelligence are the basic experiences our students get while tinkering in this lab. It is a lot similar to the DIY method. Basically the innovative ideas and skills are shaped here technically.

Every student has some passion to present in form of an art, be it handicraft or other genre. A lovely and fascinating display of the handicraft items prepared by the students of the school is a treasure in itself here. They have a planned timetable to attend the art and craft room at least once in a week to learn painting, paper-mache etc. The drawing teacher here has developed an art gallery dedicated to all religions, leaders, and national emblems.

The contemporary, traditional, and folk dances and songs are the integral part of every cultural fest at the school. The students are trained for the same in the room specially designed and furnished for this purpose. It has enough number of musical instruments and other items for the training.

The real education has no space for monotony in itself. The students get their learning spirit relaxed, refreshed, and liberated when they come to the library for reading other literary and reference books, magazines, and newspapers. This experience makes them broad-minded with an advanced approach. The librarian has adopted advanced and computer savvy method to distribute the reading material in an impartial and convenient way to all of the students.

Here every day begins with a spiritual experience of morning assembly. For it, the days are allotted to different houses so that the students can learn the spirit of team work and self reliance. The prayer ground is surrounded with many flower-beds especially of roses and marigolds. The disciplined rows of the students and their absorbed participation in the prayer make their whole day more fruitful. The news, GK questions, student talk, and teacher motivation are some regular activities of this assembly.

Apart from these above mentioned facilities, the school has set up fire extinguishers to combat any fire accident. The medical room is actively operating the first aids in case of any accidental injury. The GARIMA box, sanitary pad dispenser and disposal machines are here for girl students so that they may never feel themselves in a fix.
