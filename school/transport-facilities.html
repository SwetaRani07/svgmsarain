Transport Facilities

  • The school does not operate its own transport services such as buses or vans for students.
  • Girl students up to Class 9 are provided with cycles to ensure they have a means of transport to school.
  • Students residing outside a 15-kilometer radius from the school are eligible for a bus allowance to cover transportation costs.
  • The initiative encourages eco-friendly transportation through cycle distribution.
  • By providing allowances and cycles, the school ensures accessibility for students despite the lack of direct transport facilities.
