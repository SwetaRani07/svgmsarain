Games And Sports

  • The school offers well-maintained facilities for indoor and outdoor games, including a playground and courts for many sports.
  • Regular physical fitness sessions and activities are conducted to promote a healthy and active lifestyle among students.
  • Sports programs are designed to enhance team spirit, leadership, discipline, and strategic thinking.
  • Students actively participate in inter-school, district, state, and national-level sports events.
  • Experienced and certified coaches are available to provide professional training in various sports.
  • Equal opportunities are provided to boys and girls to participate and excel in sports activities.
  • The school organizes annual sports day and inter-house competitions to encourage participation and showcase talent.
  • Adventure sports, yoga sessions, and recreational games are incorporated into the curriculum to add diversity to physical education.
  • Outstanding athletes are awarded trophies, medals, and certificates to celebrate their achievements.
