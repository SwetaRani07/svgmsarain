Art and Crafts

  • A vibrant space that nurtures creativity and artistic expression among students.
  • Includes drawing, painting, origami, clay modeling, and craftwork.
  • Focuses on improving fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination, and imaginative thinking.
  • Spacious and well-lit rooms with all necessary tools, materials, and resources.
  • Access to a variety of materials like paints, brushes, papers, fabrics, and modeling clay.
  • Dedicated spaces to showcase student artwork, including walls, boards, and exhibition halls.
  • Encourages students to express their ideas, emotions, and stories visually.
  • Acts as a therapeutic activity to help students relax and rejuvenate.
  • Promotes understanding and appreciation of traditional and contemporary art forms.
  • Group projects teach teamwork, communication, and collective problem-solving.
  • Activities designed around festivals, seasons, and cultural events.
