Rules & Regulations

Registration Guidelines

  • Students are required to register themselves at the beginning of every academic session, including returning students.
  • Students will not be allowed to register if:
    • They have not paid earlier dues.
    • They have been rusticated due to unethical practices.
    • They have not provided proper documents.
    • They have not reached the school on time and are irregular.
  • No late registration will be allowed except for exceptional and deserving cases, at the institution's discretion.
  • Parents wishing to meet subject teachers must seek prior permission from the Principal or attend regular PTMs.
  • Students not in the prescribed uniform will not be permitted on campus.
  • Parents can meet the Principal on all working days, between 9.00 am to 12.00 noon.

Discipline Guidelines

  • All students must maintain discipline on the school campus. Gatherings outside classrooms are not permitted.
  • Wearing the prescribed school uniform is mandatory on all working days.
  • Mobile phones must remain switched off inside the campus and can only be used in emergencies with faculty permission.
  • No one may meet a student on campus without prior permission.
  • Leave applications must be submitted in advance or immediately upon return from leave in emergencies.
  • Damaging school property will result in serious consequences.
  • Unfair means during exams or assessments will not be tolerated.
  • Any changes in a student's name or contact information must be promptly reported to school authorities.
